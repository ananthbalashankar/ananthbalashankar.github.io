I'm on the job market!

I'm a final year Ph.D candidate in Computer Science advised by Prof. Lakshminarayanan Subramanian at NYU's Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences and Dr. Alex Beutel, Staff Research Scientist at Google. I work on enhancing robustness of machine learning models by incorporating domain-specific structure for applications in the domains of natural language, socio-economics, public health, and privacy. Prior to this, I worked as a Software Engineer at Google for 3 years where I worked on recommendations at Google Play Store and the Play Developer Console in Mountain View and London. In 2014, I graduated from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur with B.Tech/M.Tech in Computer Science advised by Prof. Niloy Ganguly.
